Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ACartFull ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). By using ACartFull, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We are based in Massachusetts, United States.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: your name and email address when you create an account
  • Household information: number of adults and children in your household
  • Dietary preferences: food restrictions or preferences you choose to share
  • Loyalty card numbers: store loyalty card numbers you optionally link for discount tracking
  • Pantry inventory: grocery items you manually add or scan via barcode
  • Budget and shopping goals: budget amounts, meal plans, and shopping preferences
  • Donation information: payment details when you make a Pay It Forward donation (processed securely by Stripe — we do not store full card numbers)
  • Communications: messages you send to our support team

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage data: features you use, screens you visit, and actions you take in the app
  • Location data: your approximate location (with your permission) to find nearby stores and community resources
  • Push notification tokens: to send you deal alerts and shopping reminders
  • Log data: IP address, access times, and error reports

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Store pricing data from Kroger, Flipp, and other grocery data providers
  • Product information from Open Food Facts, a public database

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the ACartFull service
  • Generate personalized grocery carts and store price comparisons
  • Match your grocery needs against this week's sale items at nearby stores
  • Send push notifications for deal alerts and shopping reminders (only with your permission)
  • Process donations through our Pay It Forward feature
  • Connect you with local community resources such as food pantries and SNAP enrollment sites
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party companies that help us operate the Service, including Supabase (database), Stripe (payments), Expo/EAS (app delivery), and Kroger/Flipp (pricing data). These providers are prohibited from using your data for other purposes.

3.2 Community Features

Meal aid requests are shared only with verified community coordinators and are anonymous by default. Donation recipients receive only the donation amount, not your personal details.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

3.4 Business Transfers

If ACartFull is acquired or merges with another company, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your data is subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

  • Account and profile data: retained until you delete your account
  • Shopping cart and pantry history: retained for 2 years, then automatically deleted
  • Price history data: retained for 1 year
  • Donation records: retained for 7 years as required by financial regulations

5. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. Loyalty card numbers are encrypted and never shared except with the associated store for pricing purposes. We do not store full payment card numbers. In the event of a data breach, we will notify you within 30 days as required by Massachusetts law.

6. Children's Privacy

ACartFull is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Contact us at privacy@acartfull.com if you believe we have.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Export your data anytime through Profile settings or by emailing us
  • Correction: Update your information anytime within the app
  • Deletion: Delete your account through Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  • Notifications: Disable push notifications through your device settings or in-app
  • Location: Disable location access through your device settings at any time
  • California residents: Additional rights under CCPA — contact us at contact@acartfull.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notice at least 14 days before they take effect.
